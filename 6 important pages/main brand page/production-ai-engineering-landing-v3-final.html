The production gap

Every tutorial stops exactly
where production starts.

Most engineers encounter these problems after go-live. Before your next deployment, check:

Your answers get worse over time — and you don't know why

Retrieval quality degrades as your corpus grows. Without a re-indexing strategy built in from day one, you discover the problem when a stakeholder does.

You cannot tell if your RAG is actually good

No golden dataset. No RAGAS baseline. No business KPIs defined. You can only tell it's wrong when it's obviously wrong — which is far too late.

Chunking was an afterthought — and it shows

Fixed chunk sizes, hardcoded. The chunking strategy impacts retrieval quality more than your vector DB choice. Most guides skip this decision entirely.

Nobody is watching the cost or the latency

Token spend, P95 response times, hallucination rate — there are no dashboards. The first signal of a problem is a surprised stakeholder or a cloud bill.

Your ingestion pipeline meets real data and falls apart

Corrupt PDFs, schema changes, partial failures, duplicate versions. Production data is always messier than the 10-document tutorial dataset.
